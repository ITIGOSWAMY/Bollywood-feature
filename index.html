A product design concept · Netflix & Prime Video

Indian films are built on songs.
Now you can navigate them.

A single Indian film can carry six or more song sequences across three hours. When one plays on Netflix or Prime Video, you are stuck. You cannot see how long it runs, skip past it, or keep the one you loved. Two concepts fix that. Every song becomes a chapter on the timeline, yours to skip, name, or add to your library. The demand is real: a fan mockup hit 4.3K upvotes and a petition. Every finished song is a finished film.
